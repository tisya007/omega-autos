Car Maintenance Tips

How to Increase Your Car’s Lifespan

Regular servicing and oil changes go a long way in keeping your vehicle running smoothly. Check your tire pressure monthly and replace worn tires promptly.

DIY Car Wash Tips

Use gentle automotive soap, avoid washing under direct sunlight to prevent watermarks, and dry with a soft microfiber cloth for best results.

Road Safety Reminders for Drivers

Always check your mirrors and blind spots, keep a safe distance from other vehicles, and never use your phone while driving.
